Reinventing Employee Attendance with Location Tracking

With GPS technology, employers can track their employees in real-time, identify their location, monitor their movement, and track their arrival and departure times from any location. GPS technology can also be used to generate automatic attendance records, determine if employees are taking the most effective route to their destination, and ensure their safety while on the job. AttendNow provides all of the above even when the users have poor or no network and integrates with payroll and leave management.
